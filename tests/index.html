INGREDIENTS

  • Flour 300g
  • Sugar 300g
  • Eggs 3
  • Oil 1 cup
  • Vanilla Extract 2tsp
  • Salt 1pinch


METHOD

  1. Sieve the flour, baking powder and salt together in a mixing bowl.
  2. Beat the eggs, sugar and oil together in another miixing bowl.
  3. Add vanilla extract
  4. Fold in he dry mixture in the wet mixture with a spatula
  5. Grease the baking pan and add the mixture
  6. Keep in the preheated oven for 40 minutes to bake
  7. Enjoy!
